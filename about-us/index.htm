Welcome to St. Andrew's

St Andrews College of Nursing is located at the heart of Pune in a peaceful and beautiful lush green campus. St. Andrews College of Nursing is a Christian minority institution established under St. Andrews Education Foundation, Pune. St. Andrews Education foundation, Pune, is a charitable trust, established under the Public Trust Act of 1950. College started Basic B.Sc nursing course on 6th November 2006. Permission was granted by Government of Maharashtra and is affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik, recognized by Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi and Maharashtra Nursing Council, Mumbai. Intake capacity for Basic B.Sc nursing is 40 seats per year. Post Basic B.Sc nursing was started in the year 2009 with a course capacity of 30 seats per year. To add a gem on the crown of our cumulating output we are now starting post graduate nursing course from the upcoming academic year.

Read More

The college possesses spacious classrooms, well equipped labs, modern library with huge ollection of books, computer lab, hostel and transport facilities as per the recommendations of the statutory bodies.

The history of our parent N.M Wadia hospital dates back to 1912 which has always been a notable icon since its inception cumulating dedication, service and successful endeavour extending more than a century. It has now evolved into a highly sophisticated and well equipped 400 bedded super specialty hospital. Students have ample opportunity to learn and acquire the best clinical skills in our parent hospital within the campus.

St Andrews college of Nursing has embarked with well-trained, experienced and skilled teaching staff. We integrate activity based, evidence and problem based teaching and learning methodologies. Apart from drive dedication, our staff have the
uniqueness to identify the needs of the students before they themselves learn their need. With this eminent work force we capitalize upon academic preparation and make the students to become expert in acquiring knowledge and skills where we already have our greatest strengths.

Our institute has an excellent record of 100% placement in various hospitals across the country and abroad.

History

St. Andrews College of Nursing is a Christian minority institution established under St. Andrews Education Foundation, Pune. St. Andrews Education Foundation, Pune, is a charitable trust, established under the Public Trust Act of 1950. 

The College started Basic B.Sc. nursing programme on 6th November 2006, recognized by Indian Nursing Council (INC), New Delhi and Maharashtra Nursing Council (MNC), Mumbai. Permission was granted by Government of Maharashtra. The College is affiliated to Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS), Nashik

Vision

The vision of St Andrews college of Nursing lies in preparing competent practitioners, nurse administrators, nurse educators and researchers capable of giving leadership in
fulfilling the dream of a healthier tomorrow for India and being partners with shared
accountability in this domain nationally & internationally.

Mission

Preparing, caring, committed, compassionate and component professional nurses capable of rendering service, education service, education and research in the field of nursing.

PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE

Dr. David K. Pillai, President & CEO

St. Andrew’s Education Foundation

Nursing is quoted as “a profession within the health care sector focused on the care of individuals, families, and communities so that they may attain, maintain, or gain optimal health and quality of life.” So when a student chooses to take up nursing it is not only a profession he or she prepares for, but a decision to make changes in the lives of many in society. This is a noble profession in which individuals get ample opportunity to serve their community.

Every student that enrolls at St. Andrews College of Nursing is directed towards the goal to make a notable change in society. Our mission is not only to help our students refine their intellects, but also to touch their hearts and souls. Examining their values and developing ethical frameworks is what is encouraged here at St. Andrews college of Nursing. 

A few things that I would like to stress on as I welcome you are that, as Nursing professionals you must be organized and on time from day one, remember your purpose each day and motivate yourself and others around you and most important of all is, don’t forget to take care of yourself. 

Being a nursing student is difficult but you can do it. Besides being in one of the most difficult periods in your life, it is also one of the most rewarding.
I will end this message with only one thing, looking forward towards the goal ahead of you and with purpose in your heart, make this learning experience one that is worthwhile not only for you but to those who will look to you for healthcare assistance.

NURSING DIRECTOR

Brig. (Mrs.) Jannet Dean (Rtd.)

Founder Principal & Nursing Director

Nursing has evolved from an unstructured method of caring for the ill to a scientific profession. The result has been movement from the mystical beliefs of primitive times to a “High- Tech, High Touch’ era.
Nursing require a delicate balance of promoting clients independence and dependence. Nursing services have become complex. The opportunities for nursing career are limitless. A new professional may choose any number of careers including nursing practice, education, research, management, administration and even entrepreneurship.

The main goal of the college is to prepare competent, caring, compassionate and excellent, value-based nurses to meet exciting challenges of nursing profession.
I share a verse from the Bible — Mathews 5:16 “ Let your light shine before others, so that they may see your good works and give glory to your Father who is in Heaven”.
